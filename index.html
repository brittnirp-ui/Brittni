About

A little about me

I'm Brittni — a senior web developer and IT administrator based in Fresno, California. I've been writing code since I was twelve, which means I have strong opinions, sharp instincts, and a long list of things I've broken and then fixed. While other twelve-year-old coding geniuses were breaking into NASA, I was getting paid in lunch money to tweak my classmates' MySpace pages. Honest work.

By trade, I'm a Software Engineer. I make websites, I build apps, and I can probably get into your smart fridge if you've lost the password. AI tooling, automation, cybersecurity, and IT support all fall under that same umbrella for me — if it runs on logic, I can work with it.

Outside of code, I'm a maker. I draw, paint, illustrate, sew, design and cut vinyl, print custom t-shirts, make hand-designed greeting cards, and 3D print pretty much whatever the moment calls for.

I hold a B.S. in Computer Science and Software Engineering and am currently pursuing a Master's in Cybersecurity. I'm a two-time ADDY Award winner, a member of Mensa and Toastmasters, and a member of the American Physical Society's Women in STEM.

If you need it done, I can do it. That's not a slogan — it's just how I'm wired.
